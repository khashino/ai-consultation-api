Germany work visa general guidance:

A person who wants to work in Germany usually needs to check the correct visa or residence permit category.

Important factors may include:
- A valid job offer from a German employer
- Relevant education or professional qualification
- Work experience related to the job
- Salary requirements depending on the visa type
- Recognition of qualifications when required
- Valid passport and supporting documents

Backend development experience can be relevant if the applicant has a job offer or meets the requirements for a skilled worker visa or EU Blue Card.

This document is only general guidance and is not legal advice. Immigration eligibility must be checked against official German immigration rules and reviewed by a qualified expert.
